This algorithm has to do with CINE MRI, which is about taking an MRI video of the beating heart. Some knowledge of CINE is assumed here. If you don't know anything about CINE, click here. My friend Aaron Curtis conceived of the idea to make a linear model for heart motion and to use an MLE filter to do that. He also thought of applying it to a Kalman filter. I came up with some mathematical derivations to make this feasible and I thought to apply it to compressed sensing.
